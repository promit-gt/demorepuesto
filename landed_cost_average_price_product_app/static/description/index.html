Allow/Apply Landed Cost on Average Costing

Most of business process using the landed cost process as they want to log freight, insurance, transportation and other additional cost on the product via landed cost. Odoo only provides landed cost feature for FIFO costing method,Other ERP and software support Average Costing Method also works with landed cost feature.Odoo13 also will support landed cost feature with FIFO and Average Both Costing Method. For those who use Odoo12 Or Odoo11 they have a major drawback that people can only use landed cost with the FIFO costing method because for FIFO generated COGS entry will be based on the price effected at first in first out manner. In this case using Average Costing Method will be more accurate and with this app we provide solutions that landed cost also works with Average Costing Method. If you are using real-time/automated inventory valuation then also generated stock accounting(cogs) entry also consider the landed cost price effected on product expense entry.

Support Landed Cost with Average Costing

Create and validate Landed Cost for Average Costing Method.

Average Landed Cost apply on Product Price

Product cost will be automatically calculated based on the average landed cost.

Accurate Stock Valuation with Landed Cost

Product cost calculated based on average landed cost will reflect in the stock valulation, produce accurate and realtime results.
